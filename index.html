Top 3 tourist places in India

1.Manali

One of the most popular hill station in Himachal,
Manali offers the most magnificient views of the Pir Panjal
and the Dhauladhar ranges covered with snow for most parts of the year.



2.Leh Ladakh

Ladakh is a union territory in the Kashmir region of India.
Formerly falling in the state of Jammu & Kashmir.
Ladakh was administered a union territory on 31st Octomber 2019. extending from
the Siachen Glacier to the main Great Himalayas. Ladakh is a land like no other.
Dominated by dramatic land..



3.Coorg

Located amidst imposing mountains in Karnataka with a perpetually misty landscape,
Coorg is a popular coffe producing hill station. It is popular for
its beutiful green hills and the streams cutting right through them.
It also stands as a popular destination because of its culture and people.
